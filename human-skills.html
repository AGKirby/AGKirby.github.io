Human Skills

Adam Graneto's final project for Biology 103: Human Anatomy, Physiology, Disease, and Health.

This objective is "Human Skills" which is about conducting research about humans and staying safe.

Work #1 - My Personalized Safety Checklist:

My first piece of work is my media piece about my personalized safety checklist when conducting experiments, which shows my mastery of the course objective because it demonstrates that I know how to conduct research in a safe manner.

This is my personalized safety checklist that I use when doing experiments, either inside or outside:

    Laboratory/Indoor Safety:
  • Wear eye protection when working with chemicals.
  • Be cautious with loose clothing near flames.
  • Put newspapers or similar items on the table to protect it if the experiment is messy.
  • Be careful not to drop anything on the ground so my dog does not get it.
  • Always read instructions carefully.
    Field/Outdoor Safety:
  • Wear shoes to protect my feet.
  • Use sunscreen if it is a sunny day
  • Be careful not to leave a mess so my dog does not get into it
  • Be cautious with nearby animals, including deer
  • Avoid going somewhere that can lead to a potentially dangerous situation
    Both:
  • Wear pants and long sleeves to protect the skin
  • Wear gloves when necessary
  • Consider the safety of others nearby, including my dog
  • When walking around, be careful not to trip over my dog
  • Do not eat or drink when working with chemicals, animals, or waste products
  • Do not taste or smell anything used for the experiment unless absolutely sure it is safe
  • Always clean up the lab space when done
  • Wash hands well both before and after the experiment
  • Always follow safety directions
  • Communicate safety concerns with others involved
  • Always have a first aid kit nearby and easily accessible
  • Seek medical assistance if necessary
  • Add new safety procedures as needed
